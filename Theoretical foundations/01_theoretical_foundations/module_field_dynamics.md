- signals propagate,  
- states influence each other,  
- regulation occurs through gradients,  
- and no single node “acts”.

A field is:

- non‑agentic,  
- non‑intentional,  
- non‑hierarchical,  
- non‑discrete.

It is defined by **relations**, not entities.
